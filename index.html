About Us

Welcome to PAR Contractors, your premier destination for bespoke luxury home construction. With a passion for perfection and an unwavering commitment to excellence, we specialize in building custom homes that epitomize opulence and sophistication. Nestled in the heart of Punta Mita, our exclusive services cater to discerning clients who desire nothing but the best.

Our Mission

At PAR Contractors, our mission is to transform your vision of a dream home into a breathtaking reality. We pride ourselves on delivering impeccable craftsmanship and personalized service, ensuring every detail of your home is meticulously planned and flawlessly executed. From securing permits to selecting the finest furnishings, we manage every aspect of the construction process, providing you with a seamless and worry-free experience.

  • Unmatched Expertise: With years of experience in the luxury home market, our team of skilled architects, designers, and builders possess the knowledge and expertise to create homes that are both innovative and timeless.

  • Comprehensive Services: From initial concept to final touches, we offer a comprehensive suite of services including custom home construction, remodeling, and permitting. Our turnkey solutions are designed to meet your unique needs and exceed your highest expectations.

Our Services

Custom Home Construction

We specialize in building bespoke luxury homes tailored to your exact specifications. Our expert team works with you from concept to completion, ensuring every detail reflects your vision and exceeds your expectations.

Remodeling and Renovation

Transform your existing property into a luxurious retreat with our remodeling and renovation services. Whether it's a complete overhaul or specific upgrades, we deliver exceptional results that enhance both the beauty and functionality of your home.

Permitting and Regulatory Compliance

Navigating the complexities of building permits and regulations can be daunting. Our experienced team handles all aspects of permitting and ensures your project complies with all local and federal requirements, providing you peace of mind.

Interior Design and Furnishing

Complete your dream home with our professional interior design and furnishing services. While our trusted subcontractors handle the execution, we collaborate closely with them to ensure the final result perfectly complements your home's architecture and reflects your personal style.

Landscape Architecture

Enhance your home's outdoor spaces with our expert landscape architecture services. Working with skilled subcontractors, we design and oversee the construction of breathtaking exteriors, from lush gardens to serene patios and pools, ensuring both beauty and functionality.
